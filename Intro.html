About me

I am Sanskar Garg, a passionate programmer from Meerut, Uttar Pradesh, India, with 4 years of hands-on experience in coding. I completed my Bachelor of Technology with a Minor in Computer Science and Engineering from Kalinga Institute of Industrial Technology, focusing on developing skills in C Programming, C++, JAVA, Python3, HTML5, CSS3, Linux - UNIX, MySQL, Data Structures and Algorithm, OOPS, and Git.

I have completed certifications in Java Programming with Data Structures and Machine Learning, and enjoy participating in coding competitions such as the Studlence Winter Hackathon Challenge.
With a passion for programming and a desire to make a positive impact on society, I am confident in my ability to contribute to any software development team.
